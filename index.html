KALOBEYEI-YOUTH INTEGRATED FARMERS

Empowering Youths & Enriching Communities

Welcome to Kalobeyei Youth Integrated Farmers Association (KYIFA), a passionate community of young changemakers working at the heart of Kalobeyei and its surroundings. At KYIFA, we believe in the power of youth, unity, and sustainable action to transform lives and build resilient communities. Rooted in the principles of empowerment, peace, and innovation, we champion agricultural development, environmental stewardship, education, gender equity, and community wellness. Through our diverse programs, we equip young people and marginalized groups with the skills, tools, and opportunities they need to thrive — socially and economically.

Empowering Youth Through Agriculture

At KYIFA, We Work with youth leaders to grow food , restore dignity and build a resilient community dedicated to achieving food security, peace and opportunity for all.

Through partnership, environmental action and skill training, we cultivate hope and sustainable livelihoods in Kalobeyei and beyond.

What We Do

Working With Youth Leaders

Youth grow skills in leadership, farming and food advocacy while working with the community and making meaningful connections.

Feeding Our Community

With the help of youth, volunteers, and food access partners, we share farm-fresh produce with our community.

Growing Fresh Food

We grow food and host community on our farms in Turkana West and Turkana County, where we practice regenerative farming with respect for the land.

OUR IMPACT

We stay busy planting, growing food and community, harvesting,sharing, and learning with youth, and we’re proud and grateful for what we accomplished together last year.

We shared 2,000 servings of produce through food access efforts – 97% of the harvest.

We contributed 50,000 directly to our youth-fund-account for their work of growing food and developing leadership + food-justice advocacy skills.
